What went well?
Communication and listening were our strong point. As throughout the week we would message the group chat what needed to be done and who will be doing them. Our team also listened very well as many instances there will be a new idea being introduce to the project and other team members would listen carefully and add in any comments, they think that would improve the product.

Oliver

What went well?
We did well on terms of communication. Whenever anyone had a question or when certain tasks needed to be done, we would message each other through Facebook messenger. Matthew as the project manager did really well allocating tasks and keeping group members on track and up to date with our current progress through Facebook messenger.  
What could be imporved?
The first couple weeks was a bit slow as tasks were not getting done. This may be due to lack of motivation since we have just finished assignment 2 and the group members might have relaxed a bit. What we can improve on next time is maybe start the assignment earlier to reduce stress and rush later. The meetings can be better organised and productive.
At least one thing that was surprising
One thing that was surprising was that we weren’t as productive with our meetings as we were in assignment 2. I think is due to inconvenient timing with our meetings. We did not have as many meetings for assignment 3 compared to assignment 2. This was surprising because our meetings in assignment 2 was incredibly useful, organised and productive. The meetings in assignment 3 were not necessarily bad, but they were not as good as the ones in assignment 2.
At least one thing that you have learned about groups
Groups are extremely exhausting and stressful when working on our own parts to add to the overall project in a remote environment. Working remotely is already very stressful for me but working remotely while knowing that I could let people down is even more stressful.

Matthew

What went well?
Communication between group members through this project was the best part of this group project as we were constantly checking in and discussing the next part of the assignment that needed to be completed. The work ethic that was put in by all group members was also great, even at times when we were a bit behind.  
What could be imporved?
At the beginning of A3 we started off slow and therefore the end of the assignment was a rush to get everything completed. By starting our assignment earlier, we would have been able to use that time to start everything and then refine our work heading into the final week of the assignment. 
At least one thing that was surprising
One that surprised most of the group was that the photo.io free trial ran out and we were all scared that our work would be deleted.
At least one thing that you have learned about groups
By working in a group, a lot of things can be achieved with the right group of people and that everyone has a different way/mindset in completing tasks.  Within a group, everyone has their own strengths and weaknesses, and by utilising everyone strengths and building on those weaknesses, that creates a strong foundation for the project currently being worked on.
