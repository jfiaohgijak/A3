Scope and Limits

The aim of this app is to assist users to search, browse and plan trips. However, due to our limited time and knowledge, the app will be lacking in a few key features. To allow users to search for locations, airports, accommodation and etc, the app must have data on those areas that is constantly updating. To do that, we would need a lot more time and access to knowledge and data about those things such as flight and accommodation data from the companies who sell them. Another limitation are the expenses. We would need to invest in hiring people to create an app that is reliable, and have people maintaining and updating it, as well as the software and engine used to create the app. What we can do now with time we have is create a prototype with a system that suggests and recommends locations, from a pool consisting of a few locations, based on previous interactions and searches on the app.
