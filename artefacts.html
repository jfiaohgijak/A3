Key Path: Booking

A user named Kevin is planning to go to Japan for a holiday. He wants an easy and efficient way to book a trip to fly to Japan and back. He has found an app called Anywhere Travels and created an account and proceeds to book a trip to Japan.  
1a) Kevin is on the homepage, and he clicks the plus sign to start booking. 
1b) Kevin is taken to a page 1 of “start your trip” section  
2a) Kevin fills in the details in the allocated boxes: where he is flying from, where he is fly to, the travel date, the passengers and the ticket class 
2b) the boxes are filled with the relevant details 
3a) Kevin clicks on the “page 2” icon 
3b) Kevin is taken to page 2 of the “start your trip” section  
4a) Kevin fills in the details in the allocated box: accommodation  
4a) Kevin fills in the details in the allocated box: accommodation  
5a) Kevin clicks on the “page 3” icon 
5b) Kevin is taken to page 3 of the ‘start your trip” section 
6a) Kevin looks at and checks his fight ticket and accommodation summary 
6b) Displays the summary of the flight tickets and accommodation  
7a) Kevin clicks in the “pay now” option  
7b) Tip has been booked and paid for successfully  

Key Path: Cancellation of Trip

A user named Dan has booked a trip on Anywhere Travels, Dan wants to go to Vietnam from Australia and has a flight booked in 3 months. However, recently something related to work has come up and Dan is unable to go to Vietnam in 3 months and as a result, must cancel his booking.  
1. Dan is taken to the homepage of the website where he is required to login 
2. Dan will then click on the menu bar and click on the person icon at the bottom of the screen 
3. Dan is then transported to the customer support page where he can submit a ticket to the support team 
4. Dan will have to wait for 1-2 business days before he gets a reply from the support team 
5. After exchanging messages, the support team will cancel the flight and refund the money to Dan.  

Key Path: Writing a Review/Experience

Ron has recently come back from a trip to Europe, where he went to Italy. He had a wonderful time and enjoyed his 2 weeks' vacation. After coming back Ron wants to write about his experiences for other travelers.  
1. Ron is taken to the homepage of the website where he is required to login 
2. Ron will then click on the menu bar and click on “Your experience” 
3. Ron is then transported to the experience page 
4. He then is allowed to submit a review / talk about his experiences on his trip 
5. After submitting other users of the website can see Ron review.
